Project Case Study

Project Overview

A promotional landing page for Bluegrass Valentine, a San Jose-based traditional bluegrass band. Designed to showcase live recordings and provide easy contact for booking.

Problem

The band needed a lightweight, professional, and fast-loading promotional site that could be easily deployed and shared.

Solution

Created a custom, framework-free landing page focused on performance and media optimization, allowing for immediate access to audio content.

Technologies Used

  • Netlify deployment for global delivery
  • FFmpeg audio optimization for high-quality, lightweight tracks
  • AI-assisted development workflow for rapid prototyping

Deployment

The site is hosted and deployed on Netlify for reliable, fast content delivery.
